# Customer Behavior Analysis

## Objective
Analyze customer transactions and behavior to identify customer segments, purchase patterns, and churn risks.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Analysis Performed
- Data Cleaning
- Feature Engineering
- Customer Segmentation
- Purchase Pattern Analysis
- Churn Analysis
- Data Visualization

## Key Findings
- Customers were segmented into Low, Medium, and High Value groups.
- High-value customers contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Purchase behavior varied across customer groups.
- Churn risk was higher among specific customer segments.

## Recommendations
- Focus on retaining high-value customers.
- Improve engagement for medium-value customers.
- Develop targeted campaigns for at-risk customers.

## Outcome
The analysis provided insights into customer behavior and helped identify opportunities to improve customer retention and engagement.
